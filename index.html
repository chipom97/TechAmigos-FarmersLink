Why FarmersLink?

We're addressing critical challenges in Botswana's agricultural sector through innovative technology

The Challenge

Botswana faces recurring pest and disease outbreaks that devastate crops and livestock, with limited access to affordable control resources and fragmented communication among stakeholders.

Our Solution

An integrated platform providing AI-powered diagnostics, real-time monitoring, and instant access to agricultural experts, making advanced agricultural technology accessible to all.

The Impact

Enhanced agricultural productivity, improved pest management, increased youth participation, and stronger collaboration across the agricultural sector.
