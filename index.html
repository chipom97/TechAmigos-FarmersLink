Our App Solutions

Livestock Health and Disease Management

This component utilises AI-powered diagnostics, allowing farmers to upload photos, videos, or keywords to identify diseases, nutrient deficiencies, and possible treatments. The solution also includes disease outbreak alerts and provides access to veterinarians for effective containment.

Pest Monitoring and Containment

Using a neural network-powered app, the platform processes images or video frames to detect pests efficiently. The app includes a vast dataset covering various pest species, their life stages, and conditions. It provides real-time alerts for pest identification, action protocols, and veterinary notifications for timely inspections, ensuring effective pest management and preventing infestations.
